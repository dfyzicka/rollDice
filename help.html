Rollem Bot - Help This bot allows you to roll all kinds of dice in your Telegram messages. It features: - Dice rolling with /roll or /r followed by dice notation - A fate dice shortcut: /rf - Exploding dice with ! - Select the highest or lowest result with H and L SEE THE WIKI for usage instructions and examples.
